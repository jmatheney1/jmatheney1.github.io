Mystic Lake
An easy-access trail that follows Bozeman Creek along Sourdough Canyon Trail. This trail is an out-and-back, 20-mile trip.The reward for the 10-mile climb is a 10-mile downhill cruise. Great for ages 14 and up. Average trail time 3hr.

Chestnut Mountain
From the trailhead, you'll immediately begin a grueling 5-mile ascent. The difficulty of this trail lies in the grade of the climb, not the trail surface which is mostly non-technical. The forest setting is absolutely beautiful as it winds you around massive granite slabs and tight switchbacks. Every clearing in the trees will award you with breathtaking views for miles. Average trail time 3.5hr.

Bangtail Devide

If this is your first ride with us please arrive 30min before departure time for bike fitting and paperwork.
*Sunday tours are must be booked 2 weeks in advance. A 20% will be added to all Sunday tours.

Pricing Details

Tour groups of 4 or 8, all slots must be filled or paid to schedule a tour. For larger parties please contact us.

Silver packages includes: Mountain bike and helmet rental, transportation to and from trail, ice water at vehicle
Gold: Everything in Silver + sack meal, recovery snacks and sports drinks at vehicle
Platinum: Everything in Gold + substituting hot meal, trail guide accompaniment

Scheduling a Tour

Tour dates should be scheduled at least 1 week in advance. A 25% deposit is required by credit card at the time of scheduling. Deposits are nonrefundable Exception, due to a notified weather event. In this case, a credit may be applied towards future tours scheduled within 30 days of canceled tour.
