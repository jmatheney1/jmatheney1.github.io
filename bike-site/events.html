Butte 100, July 29th

A 100mi figure-eight race, on the “North Loop” and “South Loop,” guaranteeing riders never see the same trail twice. The north loop has dense woods with tough packed dirt, progressing to decomposed granite, and a high-altitude desert with heavy sand at times. The first portion of the trail will test nearly every facet of pure mountain bike skill. The South Loop is a crowd favorite with epic amounts of single track on the Continental Divide Trail, with sweeping views of the area’s mountains, and unparalleled athletic tests such as the notorious Basin Creek climb
Price - $280

Cycle the Continental Divide Trail, Aug 11th-12th

A 150mi 2-day race, An easy-access trail that follows roadways along lowland Rd. This trail is an out-and-back, 150-mile trip. The reward for the first 75-miles is you get to do it again the next day. Tent and hammock sites are provided for racers, additional sites available at $35/tent. Athletes breakfast is provided for participants
